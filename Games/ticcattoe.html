Tic Cat Toe was essentially the result of a few failed/abandoned projects which were highly over scoped for two relatively inexperienced people to make.

We sat down and came up with what we thought would be a game that we could definitely finish and release within a reasonable timeframe. Obviously we also wanted to end up with a polished project at the end.

You can currently download Tic Cat Toe on Android & iOS. You can also check out the source code here.

The art was done by Yuriy Sivers.
